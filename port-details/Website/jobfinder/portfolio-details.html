Job Finder

  • Category: Website
  • Project date: June, 2021

Description

This mobile and web system was created as a product system for ITE 309: Capstone Project and Research 1, and ITE 310: Capstone Project and Research 2.

JobFinder aims to show the effectiveness of a job finding application in providing access to job opportunities to people. It aims to study how job finding application can ease the job finding and application process in comparison to traditional methods.
