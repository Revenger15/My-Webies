PUSSM - Admin Website

  • Category: Admin Website

Description

This mobile and web system was created as a product system for ITE 309: Capstone Project and Research 1, and ITE 310: Capstone Project and Research 2.

This project will provide assistance for SSP teachers and awareness of student’s mental and physical health by analyzing student’s information they provided from their experience in online class and their situation during this time of pandemic. The vision of this project will provide awareness and maintain good health to the users. This study will develop an app that will analyze the mental and physical health of a student and help SSP teachers by putting their students' class. This application will be the partner of SSP teachers in providing student support in their respective SSP students and of students in accomplishing tasks in their SSP subject.
